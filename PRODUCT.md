# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Professionals and individuals who translate family narratives into editable genograms. They work primarily on desktop and need to understand, generate, correct, and export complex family structures without fighting the interface.

## Product Purpose

Turn written or manually entered family relationships into a clear, standards-aware genogram that remains easy to inspect and edit. Success means the diagram is structurally accurate, visually legible, and fast to refine after AI generation.

## Brand Personality

Precise, calm, thoughtfully expressive. The interface should feel like a dependable diagramming tool, with moments of personality reserved for meaningful state changes such as AI generation.

## Anti-references

Avoid marketing-site spectacle inside the editing workflow, always-on neon decoration, oversized chat surfaces, gratuitous animation, and visual effects that obscure symbols or relationships.

## Design Principles

1. Keep the diagram as the visual priority.
2. Make system state visible without blocking the task.
3. Reserve expressive motion for meaningful transitions.
4. Preserve familiar editing affordances and predictable controls.
5. Prefer clear notation and spatial legibility over decoration.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Maintain keyboard-operable controls, visible state changes that do not rely on color alone, sufficient text and control contrast, and a reduced-motion presentation that removes continuous animation while preserving status information.
